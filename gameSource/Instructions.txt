Copy ALL of these files into your main OneLife folder.



Launch the game using the OneLife+.exe



Use the left and right arrow keys to decrease or increase your FOV zoom.

Use ` (Grave key) to toggle HUD scaling with the game, or fixed at 1x zoom.

Use \ (Backslash key) to toggle displaying the Lineage Fertility panel.

Use Shift + DEL to give up on life and respawn instantly.

Use Shift + Space to generate a name.

Use Shift + <a-z> to generate a name that begins with <a-z>



Latest version always available at https://github.com/Awbz/OneLife/releases/latest
